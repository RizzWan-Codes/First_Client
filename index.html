Our Story

We started with a simple idea: make great burgers that people genuinely love—fast, fresh, and fairly priced. From our Aurangabad roots to a growing community of regulars, Globurg has become a go-to spot for bold flavours and easy comfort food. We serve students, families and office-goers around CIDCO / Prozone Mall and beyond.

What we serve

A balanced menu of veg & non-veg burgers, crispy sides, and classic beverages. Popular picks include the Veggie Burger, The American Dream Burger, Fries, Chicken Pops and Cold Coffee — plus seasonal specials and late-night favourites.
